Our Story

BORN FROM
REBELLION

STUBBORN
HERITAGE

GIRAN was forged in the fires of contradiction — where ancient royal symbolism collides with raw street energy. We don't create clothes. We create armor for the modern sovereign.

Every crest, every stitch, every gemstone is a declaration of identity. We believe luxury isn't about price tags — it's about the unyielding commitment to craft, detail, and meaning.

From the gothic typography that echoes medieval manuscripts to the heavy silhouettes inspired by military precision, GIRAN pieces are built for those who refuse to blend in.
