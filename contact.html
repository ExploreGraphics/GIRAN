Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How long does delivery take?

+

Lagos orders: 1–2 business days. Nigeria-wide: 3–5 days. International: 7–14 days via DHL Express.

What is your return policy?

+

We accept returns within 14 days. Items must be unworn with original tags. Exchanges are complimentary.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes. We ship to over 40 countries. Duties and taxes calculated at checkout based on your location.

How do I find my size?

+

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. Our fits are true to size with a structured, luxury silhouette.

Can I collaborate with GIRAN?

+

We welcome visionary creatives. Send your portfolio to collab@giran.com. We review all submissions personally.

Are your materials ethically sourced?

+

Absolutely. All leather is full-grain and ethically tanned. Gemstones are conflict-free. Craftsmanship is fair-wage.
